Top 5 Favorite Movies

  1. Interstellar
    • Mind-blowing science and time-dilation concept
    • Emotional father-daughter storyline
    • Outstanding background score by Hans Zimmer
  2. Avengers: Endgame
    • Epic conclusion to the Infinity Saga
    • Highly emotional moments and great character arcs
    • Thrilling action scenes and perfect fan service
  3. The Dark Knight
    • Legendary performance by Heath Ledger as Joker
    • Strong storyline with deep moral conflicts
    • Intense action and realistic tone
  4. Inception
    • Creative and complex dream-within-a-dream concept
    • Excellent cast and direction by Christopher Nolan
    • Mind-twisting ending that keeps you thinking
  5. RRR
    • Powerful brotherhood and emotional scenes
    • Mass action sequences and energetic performances
    • Amazing soundtrack and dance choreography
